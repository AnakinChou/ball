Changing the camera position

For setting the lights and for finding a proper camera position for our picture efficient handling of the camera is needed.
To change the camera's position click with a mouse button into the 3D graphics view and drag the mouse:
  • Left button: Rotate
  • Middle or Wheel: Zoom
  • Right button: Move view
  • Left and Right button: Rotate in the screen plane

Try to zoom very close to the peptide and very far away and compare the brightness of the scene of both camera positions.

A roughly sensible camera position can be found by centering the camera on the entire peptide: Find the widget with the name Structures and highlight the System by clicking on its name ("ARND").
Then press the right mouse button to open a context menu and press on the Focus entry.

This will enable the next step of the tutorial.
