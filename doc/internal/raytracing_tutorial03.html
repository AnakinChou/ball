Setting the Background Color

For a ray tracing setting a proper background color is black. The background color can be set in the Preferences dialog. Click the menu entry
"Edit->Preferences".
Highlight Display->Main in the Entries list on the left hand. Press the Edit button in the Background Color box to change the background color. Black is provided in the Basic colors.
Confirm your color choice by pressing OK and apply the new preferences by pressing OK in the Preferences Dialog.

This will enable the next step of the tutorial.
Press Next to continue.
